The shape of a Tealios tool

Three products, one set of convictions. These are the rules every Tealios instrument has to pass before it ships.

Local-first, by conviction

It runs on your machine and answers to you. No sign-up wall. Nothing phoning home. Your work stays where you put it.

Instruments, not apps

Pads, LCDs, tally lights. Tools you operate, with status you can read at a glance. Built for hands, not dashboards.

Inspectable and honest

Open code, plain words, scope you can actually see. If a model is in the loop, the prompt is in the loop too.

Made by a maker

Solo-built, opinionated, uncluttered. The person who shipped it is the person you talk to when something breaks.
