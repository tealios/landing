ISC · 2026.06
SPEC 10 ENTRIES
Pad bank
4 × 3 default, up to 10 × 10. Drag to swap during playback.
Waveform editor
Draggable in / out handles, snap-to-grid, zoom and pan.
Audio engine
Web Audio, sample-accurate playback.
Video sync
Drift detection between audio and video.
Transport
Play all, stop all, sync-restart.
Persistence
.dec session files. Recent deck recall on launch.
Binaries
Auto-managed yt-dlp and ffmpeg, with background refresh.
Cache
LRU disk cache. Clips referenced by a deck are protected from eviction.
Platforms
Windows, macOS, Linux. Node 22+.
